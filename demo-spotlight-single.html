July Grand Opening of Machodoc Creek Marina!

King George officials and a large number of community members welcomed the opening of the Machodoc Creek marina and its owners, Jeff and Marsha Stonehill at the July 25 ribbon cutting. The marina has undergone a lot of changes and renovations over the past year. Located just one mile off the Potomac River in King George, just south of the Harry Nice Bridge, the marina is proud to be a full service boatyard which seeks to promote a family-oriented atmosphere that will welcome all visitors.

The marin houses 64 slips, 8 of which are covered as well as some space for dry storage for the cold months. It also has 24-hour access to the ramp, and has non-ethanol fuel. The stone hill partnered with All county Marin owned by Wayne Mims who is an expert on-site mechanic. His service include technicals, bottom-painting, winterization, shrink wrapping, and her offer the M.E.D.S. Check Report,which allows a full diagnostic test on any engine and generates a comprehensive report detailing everything from the number of hours on the engine to the last time it had an oil change.

The marina is home to a rich history beginning in the 1940s when it was known as "Jack's Marina". Marsha and Jeff made major renovations and upgrade to enhance the marina's functionality. Today, it offers boat services as well as fun opportunities for family adventure through the use of kayaks, canoes, and stand up paddle boards which are available for rent.

Additional plans are underway for 2019! Jeff and Marsha are implementing plans to open a two-story cafe by 2019 for boats to stop in and for community members to come out and have a fun time. Their vision for the future is to have a dynamic and interactive space with family activities, social dance nights, outdoor movie nights, and day camp for kids focused around marin-based learning and sailing lessons.

The marin is supportive of commercial fishermen, and will work with them to conduct educational points about fishing to intrigue local youth. The marina will also hold fishing tournaments and philanthropy events to benefit our wounded veterans.

Jeff and Marsha make a great business team. Marsha has a background in mental health and focuses primarily on the development side of their marina business. Jeff, a former Charter Boat Caption, a commercial fisherman, a holder of a US Coastguard Ship Master's license, and an Advisory Board Member of the Potomac River Fisheries.
